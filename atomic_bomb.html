HOW DOES A ATOMIC BOMB WORKS

A atomic bomb works on the basis of fission , a neutron is fired at a very high speed directly into the nucleus of the atom triggering the atom to split into two different parts , during this process a massive amount of energy is released . This process encourages the split atoms to fire neutrons on other atoms too.

This is how fission works
